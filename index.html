About Me

I am a trained performer with a strong background in acting, dance, fitness, and theatre. My journey began with a Diploma in Hip-Hop Dance (2013–2014), and since then I have built a career that blends creativity, discipline, and versatility.

Alongside dance, I have been actively involved in fitness training, conducting Zumba and Aerobics classes since 2015, and pursuing my passion as a fitness enthusiast for over 9 years.

My acting career spans theatre, television, short films, music videos, and digital content — including viral projects, historical dramas, and lead roles on streaming platforms.
